Hellcaster

"A fantasy deck-builder FPS about a metalhead wizard"

last updated: 24/03/2026

Roles: 3D character artist, UI artist, Shader artist & programmer
Team size: 12
Tools used: Blender, Unity, Photoshop, Jira, GitHub
Project length: sept 2025 - dec 2025

Hellcaster is a deckbuilding rythem-based shooter, that was created as part of MMU's mock-studio 'Cheddarcat'.

I contributed mainly to the visual design of the game, helping discover the unique look the game has. My contribuitons mainly lied within 3D character art, but I also held the role of UI programmer & artist, in which I created most of the player HUD and level select screens.


My contributions:

  • player HUD concept art
  • main-menu concept art
  • skeleton enemy 3D model, textures, animations
  • bat enemy 3D model, textures, animations
  • player health bar art, shaders, programming
  • combo meter art, shaders, programming
  • boss health bar art, shaders, programming
  • damage indicators
  • level select 3D model, art, programming

As this was a University module, we were given many networking opportunities. Out of the ten projects within 'Cheddarcat', three got invited to showcase their game at the 2025 MCM Comic-Con Brimingham. We were lucky enough to be one of those groups chosen!
