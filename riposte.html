Riposte

"Fight to the death to secure victory"

ABOUT

Created in one week as part of a game-jam, the prompt was "Sports from another world".


Riposte is a multiplayer fighting game where two players battle it out until one is defeated. This was created in one week as part of a university mandated game jam. This was my first time creating a game with multiplayer in mind.
