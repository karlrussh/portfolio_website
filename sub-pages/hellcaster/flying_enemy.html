Flying Enemy

"The second enemy I produced for Hellcaster"

About

This was the second and final enemy I produced for Hellcaster. Like the skeleton, I was given concept art to adapt into a 3D model. Along with creating the model and textures, I also rigged and animated the enemy's idle, attack 1 and attack 2 states.


Model:

  • Curate a Low-Poly artsyle all subsequent models will use.
  • Define a workflow within Blender that helps create low-poly models.
  • Designed a 3D model defined by our concept artists drawings.
  • Produce a to-scale model that can be imported into Unity without issue.
  • Rig the model to prep it for animation later in the pipeline.

Textures:

  • Develop a photo-bashing workflow to help define the visual look of enemies in the game.
  • Creatively adapt the concept artists sketches into a texture sheet.
  • Limit the colours and resolution of the textures to help produce a low-poly aesthetic.
