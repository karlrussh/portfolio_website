Level Select

"A diegetic approach to a level select screen"

About

The level select screen was originally developed by another team member and originally took on a more generic visual style. However, at the team lead's request, I was added to the project to help develop the level select screen’s visual identity. I took the interface in a more interactive direction by making the interface a physical object in the game world, which you could interact with and change by pressing a button. In this case, I stylised this element around a CD case to stay within the game's musical theme. The level list is placed on the left pamphlet to emulate a track list. Once pressed, the disc on the right updates to reflect the pressed level. To enter the level, the player must hover their mouse over the disc, pop it out of the case, and press on it to play. This emulates the motion of selecting a physical CD to listen to music.

  • Worked closely with the previous team member to consolidate my front-end design into their back-end code.
  • Developed a diegetic and stylish approach to a normally generic piece of user interface.
  • Programmed interactive elements such as camera sway, animations and shaders.
  • Helped consolidate a user interface element into the game's broader theming.
  • Iterated upon design elements that were present in the previous team member's version of the interface.
